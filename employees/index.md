# Freewill Marketing Team — Operating Manuals

Seven AI employee archetypes built from the 40 skills in this repo, tailored for Freewill: a pre-launch iOS app helping people build social confidence through CBT-based real-world challenges.

Each manual is designed to run on autopilot — hand it to an AI agent or new hire and they can execute without supervision.

## How to Use These Manuals

**As a solo founder (Tyler):** Rotate through these roles throughout the week. Each manual has a daily procedure (30 min) and a weekly procedure (2–4 hrs). You can't do all 7 daily — prioritize based on your current phase:

| Phase | Priority Employees (daily) | Secondary (weekly only) |
|-------|---------------------------|------------------------|
| TestFlight | Lifecycle & Retention, Growth Strategist | Content, Search & Discovery |
| App Store Soft Launch | Conversion Architect, Search & Discovery, Content | Paid Acquisition, Revenue Ops |
| Product Hunt Launch | Growth Strategist, Content, Lifecycle & Retention | All others |
| Growth | All daily procedures | — |

**As AI agent prompts:** Each manual can be used as a system prompt for an AI agent. Give the agent the manual + the product marketing context doc + access to the relevant tools, and it can execute the daily and weekly procedures autonomously.
